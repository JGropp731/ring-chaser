RingChaser

Build an all-time NBA starting five from random rolls — and chase chemistry from shared colleges and real teammates.

How it works

  • 🎲 Each round, Roll a random franchise + season.
  • You must fill five positions: PG, SG, SF, PF, C. You can only draft a player whose spot is still open — so a roll full of guards is useless once both guard slots are filled. That's the challenge.
  • ↻ One Team re-roll and one Year re-roll per round.
  • ⚡ Chemistry: same college = +3, real teammates = +5 — and it converts straight into extra wins.
  • Your five become Team Power → a record out of 82. Going 82-0 takes elite players and stacked chemistry.
