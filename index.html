RingChaser

Build an all-time NBA starting five from random rolls — one player per spot (PG–C) — and chase chemistry from shared colleges and real teammates.

How it works

  • 🎲 Each round, Roll a random franchise + season.
  • You must fill five positions: PG, SG, SF, PF, C. You can only draft a player whose spot is still open — so a roll full of guards is useless once both guard slots are filled. That's the challenge.
  • ↻ You get one Team re-roll and one Year re-roll for the entire game — spend them wisely.
  • ⭐ OVR blends all five box-score stats into a 40–99 rating: PTS + 1.2×REB + 1.5×AST + 3×STL + 3×BLK. Defense and playmaking are weighted heavily, so an all-around big like Jokić rates 99 while a pure scorer rates lower. Your record = the team's combined value + chemistry.
  • ⚡ Chemistry (separate from OVR) links players who connect, and every link draws a labeled line on the court:
    • NBA teammates +5 — solid line
    • College teammates +3 — dashed
    • Same school (different eras) +1 — dotted
    Bonuses stack (e.g. Villanova + NBA teammates), and chemistry adds wins.
  • Your five become a record out of 82. Going 82-0 takes elite OVR and stacked chemistry.
